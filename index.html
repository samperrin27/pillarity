How it works
Three steps to finding identical medicines
📋

1. Regulatory data

We pull formulation data for every licensed UK medicine from the Electronic Medicines Compendium (EMC) — the official source used by pharmacists and doctors.

🔬

2. Formula matching

We compare products using a 7-tier hierarchy — from PL code matching (100% identical) down to excipient analysis — to identify when two products are the same formula.

💊

3. Price comparison

We show you where identical products are sold at different prices across UK retailers, so you can make an informed choice without compromising on quality.

About Pillarity

Pillarity was built to bring transparency to the UK medicines market. Using data from the Electronic Medicines Compendium — the same source used by NHS pharmacists — we compare the formulations of licensed medicines to identify when branded and own-brand products are genuinely identical.

Pillarity is independent and has no affiliation with any pharmaceutical company or retailer.
